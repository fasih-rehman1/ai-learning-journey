# My AI Map  Day 1

## 1. Artificial Intelligence (AI)
Definition:** The broad goal of making machines perform human-like tasks.
Real Product Example:** Tesla Autopilot (which uses cameras and sensors to navigate).

## 2. Machine Learning (ML)
Definition:** A subset of AI where systems learn patterns from data instead of relying on explicit rules.
Real Product Example:** Netflix Recommendation Engine (which learns which movies you like based on your viewing history).

## 3. Deep Learning (DL)
Definition:** A subset of ML using multi-layered neural networks to automatically discover features from raw data.
Real Product Example:** Google Photos Face Recognition (which learns to identify "eyes," "nose," and your specific face pattern automatically from millions of photos).

## 4. Generative AI (GenAI)
Definition:** A subset of DL that can create brand new content (text, images, code) from learned patterns.
Real Product Example:** ChatGPT (which generates human-like text responses to prompts).

## Senior Engineer Takeaway
The LLM is just an API call in my code. My actual job is the software engineering infrastructure around it: input validation, error handling, observability, and cost management.
